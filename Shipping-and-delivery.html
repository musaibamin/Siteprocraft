Shipping and Delivery Policy

Welcome to SiteProCraft! Our Shipping and Delivery Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which we deliver our products to you. By purchasing from our website, you agree to the terms specified in this policy. Please read this information carefully to understand our practices regarding your orders.

1. Order Processing

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days after receiving your order confirmation email. You will receive another notification when your order has shipped. Please note that orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays. If we are experiencing a high volume of orders, shipments may be delayed by a few days. Please allow additional days in transit for delivery. If there will be a significant delay in shipment of your order, we will contact you via email or telephone.

2. Shipping Rates and Delivery Estimates

  • Domestic Shipping: We offer a flat rate for shipping within the country. The estimated delivery time for standard shipping is 5-7 business days.
  • International Shipping: We provide international shipping to selected countries. Shipping rates and delivery times vary depending on the destination. The estimated delivery time for international shipping is 10-15 business days.
  • Expedited Shipping: Expedited shipping options are available at an additional cost. The estimated delivery time for expedited shipping is 2-3 business days.

3. Shipment Confirmation and Order Tracking

You will receive a shipment confirmation email once your order has shipped containing your tracking number(s). The tracking number will be active within 24 hours. You can track the status of your order through the provided tracking link.

4. Customs, Duties, and Taxes

SiteProCraft is not responsible for any customs and taxes applied to your order. All fees imposed during or after shipping are the responsibility of the customer (tariffs, taxes, etc.). Customs policies vary widely from country to country; please contact your local customs office for more information. If you refuse a shipment due to unexpected import fees, the cost of the original shipping, any duties, taxes, and/or customs charges will not be refunded.

5. Damages and Lost Items

SiteProCraft is not liable for any products damaged or lost during shipping. If you received your order damaged, please contact the shipment carrier to file a claim. Please save all packaging materials and damaged goods before filing a claim.

If you believe your order is lost, please contact us at contact@siteprocraft.com. We will assist you in tracking down the shipment. Please note that once an item is marked as "delivered" by the shipping carrier, SiteProCraft is not responsible for lost or stolen packages.

6. Returns and Refunds

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you may be eligible for a return or refund according to our Cancellation and Refund Policy. Please review this policy for details on how to initiate a return or request a refund.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Shipping and Delivery Policy, please contact us at contact@siteprocraft.com. We are here to assist you and provide the best shopping experience possible.
